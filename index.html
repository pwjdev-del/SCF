Frequently Asked Questions

As part of SCF Lifelong Learning and Workforce Development, LearnEd offers a variety of non-credit courses, workshops, and programs that are open to the public. You do not need to enroll in the College in order to participate. You can take as many courses as you want, whenever you want.

Participants of all ages are welcome. We offer programs, workshops, camps, and courses for everyone from elementary school children to retirees.

While LearnEd is a division of State College of Florida (SCF), enrollment at SCF is not required to participate. All LearnEd courses, camps, and workshops are open to the public.

Fees vary based on the type of course, level of instruction, and materials needed. Since LearnEd is a self-supported division of SCF, course pricing is set separately from the for-credit courses at the College. Please note that all fee payments are due at the time of enrollment via credit or debit card.

Courses are available in person, online, or in a hybrid format. In-person courses take place at SCF Bradenton, SCF Lakewood Ranch, and SCF Venice, with select courses offered in Downtown Sarasota. For customized corporate training, we offer onsite at your organization as well.

We understand that sometimes life happens. For multi-session classes, you must submit a cancellation request in writing to LearnEd@SCF.edu at least five (5) business days prior to the first class. For single-session workshops, the deadline is three (3) business days. Contact us at LearnEd@SCF.edu for full policy details.
