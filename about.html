About LearnEd

Connections That Ignite Unlimited Opportunities for Lifelong Learning.

Our Mission

Empowering Learners at Every Stage

As a division of State College of Florida, LearnEd has been empowering individuals and businesses with non-credit programs, camps, and workshops that create meaningful change in our community since 2002.

Our programs span everything from youth STEAM camps and adult enrichment courses to professional certifications and corporate training. Whether you're looking to spark creativity in your child, advance your career, explore a new hobby, or train your team, LearnEd has something for you.

We believe learning never stops. That's why we design accessible, engaging programs that fit your life—offered in person at our three campuses, online from anywhere, or through customized on-site training for organizations.

What We Value

The LearnEd Approach

Community First

We're deeply rooted in Manatee and Sarasota counties, serving our neighbors with programs designed around local needs and interests.

Quality Instruction

Our courses are taught by industry professionals, subject matter experts, and passionate educators who bring real-world experience to every lesson.

Accessible Learning

Flexible formats, convenient locations, and programs for every budget make learning accessible to everyone in our community.

Practical Skills

Every program is designed to deliver real, applicable skills you can use immediately—whether at work, home, or in your community.

Lifelong Learning

We believe growth happens at every age and stage. Our programs support continuous personal and professional development.

Trusted Partner

Backed by State College of Florida, we bring institutional excellence and resources to community education.
